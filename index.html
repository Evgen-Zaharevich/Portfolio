Hello! My name is Evgen, and I am a beginner in the field of frontend development. I invite you to learn a little more about me.
Always had a passion for technology and building the web world. My web development journey started with small HTML and CSS projects that quickly turned into real pages with live design. During this time, I learned the most important principles of layout, as well as the basics of responsive design, which allows you to create websites that look great on any device.
I am always open to new challenges and ready to learn every day. I believe that the best way to learn something is to practice it in real projects. My goal is to grow as a developer and improve my skills to create modern and exciting web applications.
I am always happy for new opportunities and cooperation with interesting people. Feel free to contact me to collaborate or just to discuss web development.
Thank you for visiting my portfolio and I hope to have the opportunity to work with you!
